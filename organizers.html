Get to know us

We are as a Net Summit: advanced large-scale technology and business events organizer that brings the leading experts in the IT, Innovation, FinTech and Banking industries together from all around the world.

So far we have held several industrial events concerning Banking, Fintech, Tourism, Retail, Influencer and Oil-Gas sectors

The annual Monex Summit which is a result of the natural continuation of our proven track record in delivering large-scale international events brings together the Fintech and Bank leaders. So far it has been held in Baku, Frankfurt and Warsaw, and it is going to be the 5th edition of Monex Summit to be held within the World Net Summit together with Global Influencer Day- a global Congress that has brought together the industry experts, tech leaders such as Sophia the Robot, Facebook, Google, influencers, engagers, brand and business owners together to discuss trending topics in the realm of Influencer Marketing.

Besides Monex Summit and Influencer day, there will be 6 more phases to be held within WNS that are Government Address, AutoTech Conf, StartUp Academy, Venture Summit, Corporate Innovation Forum. You can get to know more about them by clicking the "Phases" button.

Innovational century

This century has been full of innovation. New technologies, new products, new services, whole new industries have emerged. Yet the call for innovation in business has never been more intense. We come up with topics that need to be discussed and issues that need to get some serious attention. We create an ideal environment for networking between suppliers, providers and customers.
